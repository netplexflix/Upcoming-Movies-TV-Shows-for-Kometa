1. Download the Ratings overlay files (r0.png-R100.png) and NEW.png and place them in your kometa/config/overlays folder
2. Place config.yml and tssk_config.yml in your UMTK/config folder. Enter your Radarr, Sonarr and MDBList API keys.
3. Add "- url: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/netplexflix/Overlays/refs/heads/main/TSSK-Kabeb/TSSK-Kabeb.yml" to the overlay_files section of your TV library in your Kometa config
